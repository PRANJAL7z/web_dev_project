TECHNICAL DETAILS

Algorithm: Linear Regression (scikit-learn)
Formula: y = mx + b
  • m = coefficient (slope)
  • b = intercept
  • x = crime count
  • y = predicted score

R² Score: Model accuracy
  • 1.0 = Perfect fit
  • 0.5 = Moderate fit
  • 0.0 = No correlation
